Caprese Appetizer

Best Caprese Skewers

These colorful caprese skewers with bite-sized portions of mozzarella cheese, tomato, and basil make the perfect appetizer for holiday parties.

See Nutrition Facts

Prep Time: 15 mins

Total Time: 15 mins

Serving: 10

Ingredients

1X

  • 20 cherry tomatoes
  • 10 ounces mozzarella cheese, cubed
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 20 toothpicks

2X

  • 40 cherry tomatoes
  • 1 ¼ pounds mozzarella cheese, cubed
  • 4 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 4 tablespoons fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 2 pinches salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
  • 40 toothpicks

4X

  • 80 cherry tomatoes
  • 2 ½pounds mozzarella cheese, cubed
  • 8 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 8 tablespoons fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 4 pinches salt
  • 4 pinches ground black pepper
  • 80 toothpicks

Directions

Step 1

Gather all ingredients.

Step 2

Toss tomatoes, mozzarella, olive oil, basil, salt, and pepper together in a bowl until well coated.

Step 3

Skewer one tomato and one piece of mozzarella on each toothpick.
