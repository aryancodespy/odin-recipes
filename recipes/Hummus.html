Super Easy Hummus

Hummus Recipes

This homemade hummus recipe is incredibly easy to make in minutes with canned chickpeas. Great with veggies or pita chips. Really it's great with anything!

See Nutrition Facts

Prep Time: 5 mins

Total Time: 5 mins

Serving: 4

Ingredients

1X

  • 1 (15 ounce) can chickpeas, drained, liquid reserved
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • ½ teaspoon ground cumin
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 drops sesame oil, or to taste (Optional)

2X

  • 2 (15 ounce) can chickpeas, drained, liquid reserved
  • 2 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 drops sesame oil, or to taste (Optional)

4X

  • 4 (15 ounce) can chickpeas, drained, liquid reserved
  • 4 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 4 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 clove garlic, crushed
  • 2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoon salt
  • 8 drops sesame oil, or to taste (Optional)

Directions

Step 1

Gather all ingredients.

Step 2

Blend chickpeas, lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, cumin, salt, and sesame oil in a food processor.

Step 3

Stream reserved chickpea liquid into the mixture as it blends until desired consistency is achieved.

Step 4

Serve with pita chips or veggies.
